About Ame

Ame Houston is a self-taught painter/multi-media artist.
Originally from Metro Detroit, Ame has called East Tennessee home since 2019.
She lives and works in a little green house with her 3 little cats and lover.
Her work focuses on urban decay and gentrification, with Love Letters being her latest series.
To see more, find her on Instagram @junedogart

Specialties & Skills

Painting

A self-taught painter, Ame primarily works in acrylic and gouache. Her work involves urban spaces, the natural world, and the effect that time has on human structures.

Printmaking

Ame's print work involves linocuts of insects and plants, which she can then use for a variety of purposes - from shirt prints, to collage work.

Web Development

Ame's formal education is in software engineering, with a focus on front-end web development. She is interested in web accessibility, and creating meaningful digital experiences.
